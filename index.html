Science

I love science because it helps us understand the world and universe around us in ways that were once unimaginable. One of the things that fascinates me most is space exploration. The Hubble Space Telescope for example, since its launch in 1990, has changed our fundamental understanding of the universe. It has made it possible to determine the atmospheric composition of planets around other stars to discovering dark energy.

One of its most popular images, the Hubble Ultra Deep Field, shows nearly 10,000 galaxies. This snapshot includes galaxies of various:

  • Ages
  • Sizes
  • Shapes
  • Colours

The smallest, reddest galaxies in the image, about 100, may be among the most distant known, existing when the universe was just 800 million years old.
